Rulebook

Quick briefing

Clean all four sectors before your term ends. The safest path is to combine cleanup with permanent legislation. Research and journal progress are permanent across runs, so every win makes future attempts easier.

1. Win condition

Clean every sector. For the best ending, also pass permanent anti-runoff legislation before time runs out.

2. Robot cleaning

Fast and safe for wildlife. Most reliable option, but it costs the most money.

3. Filter cleaning

Balanced and stable. Safer than chemicals, but it takes the longest to fully deploy.

4. Chemical cleaning

Cheap and powerful, but this is the only cleanup method that can trigger civil unrest. It also causes ecological damage unless reduced by research.

5. Environmental disasters

Storms, floods, and freeze events can re-pollute sectors or slow operations. Watch the news roll and react fast.

6. Permanent progression

Winning grants skill points. Spend them in the research tree, and archive discoveries in the journal to earn permanent bonuses across future runs.
